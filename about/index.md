# What is VUnit?

VUnit is an open source unit testing framework for VHDL released under the terms of Mozilla Public License, v. 2.0. It features the functionality needed to realize continuous and automated testing of your VHDL code. VUnit doesn't replace but rather complements traditional testing methodologies by supporting a "test early and often" approach through automation. [Read more](https://github.com/VUnit/vunit/blob/master/README.rst).
